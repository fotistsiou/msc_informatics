Form 1

The home page is the only way to get to the program's three different apps. This means that if we enter one of the three applications, to switch to another, we will have to go from the corresponding button on our home page and then switch to the other application we want.

At the top it has two buttons, on which with hover we can see a spoken explanation of the button. Clicking the first button exits the application, while clicking the second button displays the help window.



Finally there are three wider buttons which lead us to the corresponding application of the program.
